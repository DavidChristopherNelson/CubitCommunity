HOW IT WORKS

Wooden beams 5cm by 5cm thick supports the gap between the plywood sheets that can be filled with insulation, plumbing and wiring.

Plumbing and wiring can be built into the panel enabling simple plug in connections avoiding the need for plumbers and electricians.

Structural plywood sheets are used in construction to provide torsion and shear strength. These panels are bolted to the steel frame.

The steel square tubes are bolted to three different types of connectors, one for each kind of corner.

The 1m steel square tubes provide great compression strength. The rubber on flange on the edge provides a water proof seal.
